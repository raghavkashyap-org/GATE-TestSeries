Q7 MSQ Not checked
Technical • COMPUTER SCIENCE • +2 / -0
Not attempted

The degree sequence of a simple graph is the sequence of the degree of the nodes in the graph in decreasing order. Which of the following sequence can be the degree sequence of any graph?

Correct answer: A, B
Solution

(a, b)
Using Havell-Hakimi algorithm:
(i) Remove top node of the sequence.
(ii) Substract 1 from as many nodes in remaining sequence as the degree of top node that was removed.
(iii) Rearrange the sequence in non-increasing order.
(iv) Check if result sequence is a graph.
(v) Proceed again step (i)
If the given sequence is not a graph. We will see a violation in step (iv). Such as presence of negative degrees in the sequence. Otherwise the algorithm will bottom out with a degree sequence consisting of only even number of 1’s and any number of 0’s.
