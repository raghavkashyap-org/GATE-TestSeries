Not attempted

A computer system has a cache memory with a hit ratio of 98% and an access time of 2 ns. If the miss penalty is 20 ns, what is the effective access time of the cache memory?

Correct answer: A
Solution

(a)
Effective access time = Hit ratio × Hit time + (1 – Hit ratio) × Miss penalty
Effective access time = (0.98 × 2 ns) + (0.02 × 20 ns)
= 1.96 ns + 0.4 ns = 2.36 ns
