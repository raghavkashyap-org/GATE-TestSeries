Correct answer: A, B, C, D
Solution

(b) Median binning would replace each value with the median, not the mean. So, option (b) is incorrect.
(c) Equal-width binning refers to dividing the data range into equal-sized bins, not replacing values with the mean. So, option (c) is incorrect.
(d) Frequency binning refers to bins containing equal numbers of data points, not mean replacement. So, option (d) is incorrect.
