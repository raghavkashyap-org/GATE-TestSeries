Correct answer: B
Solution

(b)
If you merge two classes, the total number of classes K decreases by 1, which reduces the number of parameters that need to be estimated. Specifically, for the Naive Bayes classifier:
The number of class priors decreases from K to K – 1.
The number of conditional probabilities decreases from K × D × V to (K – 1) × D × V.
