S1 : Delete kth element in linked list.
S2 : Reverse the elements of linked list.
Which of the following represent the time complexity of above two operations respectively?

Correct answer: A
Solution

(a)
Operation 1
• Search kth element in array take Ο(1) time.
• Delete take Ο(1) time
• Shift all element to left if space is there Ο(n)

• Reversal of linked list will take Ο(n) time since circular array has to follow the properties of
linked list.
