Correct answer: A
Solution

(a)

In Lasso Regression, what does the penalty term added to the cost function encourage?

Correct answer: A
Solution

(a)
In Lasso Regression, the penalty term (L1 regularization) encourages sparsity in the coefficients by adding the sum of absolute values of coefficients to the cost function. This leads to some coefficients being exactly zero, effectively performing feature selection.

Correct answer: C
Solution

(c)
S1 : Linear support vector machine is a parametric because it can only produce linear boundaries.

So, S2 is true.
