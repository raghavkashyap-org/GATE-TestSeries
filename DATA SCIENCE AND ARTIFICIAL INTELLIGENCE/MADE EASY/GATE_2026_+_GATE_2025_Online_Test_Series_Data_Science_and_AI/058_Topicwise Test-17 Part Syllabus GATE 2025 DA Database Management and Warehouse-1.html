For two relations R(A,B,C) and S(D,E) , primary key of R is A, primary key of S is E and D of S is foreign key referencing to R. Consider the following statements:

(a) Each record of R is related to 0 or more record of S.
(b) Each record of S is related to 0 or more record of R.
(c) Each record of S is related to 0 or 1 record of R.
(d) Each record of R is related to 0 or 1 record of S.
Which of the following is/are true?

Correct answer: A, C
Solution

(a, c)
Option (a) is correct because relation S is allowed to take only those value which are part of relation R. Hence, R is related to 0 or more record of S. Option (c) is correct, since relation S is going to have only those values which are part of R. Hence, it is related to only 1 value of R. But the field with NULL is related to 0 value of R.
Hence, option (b) and (d) are false.
