Correct answer: A
Solution

(c)

Q15 MCQ Not checked
Technical • Data Science & Artificial Intelligence • +1 / -0.33
Not attempted

Consider the following statements:
S1 : The null hypothesis is always rejected if the p-value is less than the significance level.
S2 : A one-tailed test can only test for deviations in one direction (either greater than or less than).
Which of the following is true?

Correct answer: B
Solution

(b)
• If the p-value is less than the significance level, we reject the null hypothesis. However, we do
not always reject it — this is just the decision rule. So, S1 is false.
• A one-tailed test only tests for deviations in one direction, either greater than or less than.
So, S2 is true.
